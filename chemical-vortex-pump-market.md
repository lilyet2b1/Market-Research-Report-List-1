<p><ul><li>Closed Vortex Pump</li><li>Open Vortex Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical vortex pump market is primarily categorized into closed vortex and open vortex pumps. Closed vortex pumps utilize a sealed design to enhance efficiency and minimize fluid contamination, making them suitable for hazardous chemicals. In contrast, open vortex pumps operate with an exposed impeller, allowing for easier handling of submerged solids and larger particulates. Both types serve distinct applications in chemical processing, with closed vortex pumps focusing on safe, clean transport and open vortex pumps emphasizing versatility and robust handling capabilities.</p></p>
