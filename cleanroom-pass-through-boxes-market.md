<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cleanroom pass-through boxes are critical for maintaining sterile environments in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. The market can be categorized into static and dynamic types. Static pass-through boxes are designed for simple transfer of materials without airflow control, ensuring minimal contamination risk. In contrast, dynamic pass-through boxes incorporate airflow management systems that create a controlled environment, often featuring HEPA filters to maintain air purity. Each type serves specific operational needs, enhancing cleanliness and efficiency in cleanroom protocols.</p></p>
