<p><p>Driver Monitoring Systems (DMS) encompass various technologies aimed at enhancing road safety by assessing driver behavior. Fatigue monitoring systems detect drowsiness through physiological indicators, while distraction monitoring focuses on identifying inattention caused by external stimuli. Eye tracking technology analyzes eye movement to determine engagement levels. Call monitoring assesses driver interaction with mobile devices during calls, and eating monitoring identifies when drivers are consuming food, potentially diverting their attention. Together, these systems aim to reduce accidents caused by driver impairment or distraction.</p></p>
