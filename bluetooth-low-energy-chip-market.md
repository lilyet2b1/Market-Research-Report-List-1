<p><p>The Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) chip market is characterized by distinct types based on data transmission rates. Enhanced Data Rate (EDR) chips provide improved data speeds, suitable for applications requiring higher bandwidth while maintaining low energy consumption. High Speed (HS) or Alternate MAC/PHY (AMP) chips achieve even faster data rates by using an alternate physical layer, allowing larger data transfers efficiently. Both types focus on maximizing performance while minimizing battery use, catering to diverse applications in IoT, wearables, and smart devices.</p></p>
